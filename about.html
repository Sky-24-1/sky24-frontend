About Sky24

Sky24 is an independent online real-estate listing platform created to help property buyers connect directly with local property brokers and agents across India.

Our goal is to provide a simple, transparent, and completely free platform where brokers can list properties for sale or rent, and buyers can explore available properties and contact brokers directly.

Sky24 does not act as a broker, agent, or intermediary in any transaction. We do not participate in negotiations, pricing decisions, payments, agreements, or property documentation.

All brokers listed on Sky24 are independent professionals. Sky24 does not employ or represent any broker or agent.

Sky24 is a service provider that offers a digital platform to connect buyers and brokers. All property-related dealings are conducted independently between the buyer and the broker.

Sky24 is a free real estate listing platform designed to connect buyers and local brokers across India. Our goal is to simplify property discovery by offering a transparent, easy-to-use platform.

Sky24 does not own, sell, rent, or broker any property. We do not charge any fees for listings. All property information is provided directly by independent brokers and owners.

Sky24 only acts as a technology service provider to help users discover properties and contact brokers.
