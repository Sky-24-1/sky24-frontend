How Sky24 Works

1. How to Register

Users can register on Sky24 either as a Buyer or a Broker.

  • Buyers need to provide basic details like name, email, and mobile number.
  • Brokers must provide additional details such as address, Aadhaar card (front & back), and a passport-size photo.

2. How to Login

After registration, users can log in using their registered email or username and password.

3. How Brokers List Property

  • Login as a broker
  • Click on Add Property
  • Fill property details such as location, price, size, and property type
  • Upload property photos (hall, kitchen, bedrooms, bathrooms)
  • Submit the listing

All property listings are reviewed and can be managed by the broker from their account.

4. How Buyers Search Property

  • Select State → City → Area
  • Or search by property type
  • Click on any property to view full details

5. How to Search a Broker

Each broker has a unique Broker ID. Buyers can search properties listed by a broker using this Broker ID.

6. Forgot Password

If you forget your password:

  • Click on Forgot Password on the login screen
  • Enter your registered email
  • Follow the instructions sent to your email

7. Important Note

Sky24 is only a technology platform. We do not take part in any property transaction, payments, or documentation between buyers and brokers.
